Our Story

Passion for Perfect Spaces

Founded in 2014, R&D Dream was born from a simple belief: every person deserves a space that truly reflects who they are. What began as a small studio in Durgapur has grown into one of West Bengal's most celebrated interior design firms.

Our team of 25+ designers, architects, and craftspeople bring together decades of combined experience, a relentless pursuit of perfection, and an innate understanding of what makes a space feel truly alive.

Our Mission

To transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences by combining innovative design, premium craftsmanship, and a deep understanding of each client's unique lifestyle and aspirations.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted luxury interior design studio across Eastern India, setting new benchmarks for design excellence, client satisfaction, and sustainable craftsmanship.
